Frequently Asked Questions

How does SyllabusAI analyze my syllabus?

SyllabusAI uses advanced AI models to read your course documents and identify key dates, tasks, and topics. It then structures them into a cohesive study plan based on your preferences.

Do you support all file formats?

We currently support PDF, DOCX, and plain text files. Most university syllabi are provided in these formats.

Is there a mobile app?

SyllabusAI is a progressive web app, meaning it works perfectly on your mobile browser. Dedicated iOS and Android apps are coming soon!
